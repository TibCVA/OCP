Testing demand curve: Peak at 3–10 MW just before FID

Testing needs escalate from prototype cell (<0.5 MW) to industrial-scale pilot (>5 MW), following CSIRO HyResource project cycle definitions.

Third-party testing demand emerges 12–18 months before FID (typically 3–10 MW). Demand decreases post-FID, with periodic O&M checks.

Global panorama 2025: Only ~15 MW open testing capacity (>1 MW) operational

Currently no publicly accessible platform exceeds 10 MW, contrasting sharply with announced multi-100 MW projects.

Aggregate open-access test capacity (~15 MW) meets <0.3 % of demand from announced multi-100 MW scale projects (IEA Global Hydrogen Review 2024).
